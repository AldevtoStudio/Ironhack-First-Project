King's Tower

Keep the king above fire.

Controls

You can move using WASD if you're inside any window. To throw the king you need to position him on the window's edge.

Click the king with the left mouse button, drag into the opposite direction him to jump and release the mouse button. What a jump!

Tips

If any cannonball hits the king, he will fall down. The king can bounce on the tower walls and also on the enemies head. Poor pigs!
