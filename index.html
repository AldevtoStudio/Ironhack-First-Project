King's Tower

Keep the king above fire.

Controls

You can move using WASD if you're inside any window. To throw the king you need to be in any of the window borders.

Click the king with the left mouse button, drag in the opposite direction you want to jump and release the mouse button. What a jump!

Tips

If any cannonball hits the king, it will fall down. The king can bounce in the tower walls and also in the enemies head. Poor pigs!
