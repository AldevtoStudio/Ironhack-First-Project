King's Tower

Keep the king above fire.

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move if you are inside any window. To throw the king
you need to position him on
the window's edge.

Click the king with the left mouse button, drag into the opposite direction you want to jump and release the mouse button. What a jump!

Tips

If any cannonball hits the king, he will fall down and die. The king can bounce on the tower walls and also on the enemies head. Poor pigs!
